    notes on PBD:

    • 'Position based' is relative to 'force based'.
    • PBD updates position first, while force-based methods update velocity first.
    • Update of position is based on projection (a map from to ).
    • See 'A Survey on Position Based Dynamics, 2017' for more detail.
