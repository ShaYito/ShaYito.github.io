    Volume_Rendering

    in NeRF-2020

    color \(C(\pmb r)=\int_{t_n}^{t_f} T(t) \sigma (\pmb r(t)) \pmb c(\mathbf r(t),\pmb d) dt\), where transmittance \(T(t)=exp(-\int_{t_n}^{t_f} \sigma (\pmb r(s))ds)\), ray \(\pmb r(t)=\pmb o+t \pmb d\).

    radiance field 指 \(c(\mathbf r(t),\pmb d)\)

    stratified sampling for integral estimation:

    • do random uniform sample in each even partition \([t_n, t_f]\). 即 \(t_i \sim U[t_n+\frac{i-1}{N}(t_f-t_N), t_n+\frac{i}{N}(t_f-t_N)]\), \(N\) is the number of partitions.

    • color \(\hat C (\pmb r)=\sum _{i=1}^{N} T_i (1-exp(-\sigma _i \delta _i)) \pmb c_i\), where \(T_i=exp(-\sum _{j=1}^{i-1} \sigma_j \delta_j)\), \(\delta_i=t_{i+1}-t_{i}\).

    注：\(\sigma (\pmb r(t))\)采样后换成了\(1-exp(-\sigma _i \delta _i)\)，利用\(exp(-x)\rightarrow 1-x (x\rightarrow 0)\)；

    注：在\(T_i\)表达式中，采样值\(\sigma_j\)乘区间长度\(\delta_i\)代替了积分。

    in Volume Rendering of Neural Implicit Surfaces-2021-Yariv

    paper page

    " In essence, volume rendering is all about approximating the integrated light radiance along this ray reaching the camera. "

    " 体渲染的本质：估计积分 "

    transparency \(T(t)=exp(-\int_{t_n}^{t_f} \sigma (\pmb r(s))ds)\) (same as NeRF)

    opacity \(O(t)=1-T(t)\), \(O(0)=0\), \(O(\infty)=1\), and it is monotonic increasing.

    thus \(\tau (t)=\frac{dO(t)}{dt}=\sigma(\pmb x(t))T(t)\) is a PDF (Probability Density Function)

    light \(I(\pmb r)=\int _0 ^\infty L(\pmb r(t), \pmb n(t),\pmb d)\tau(t) dt\), where \(L(\pmb r(t), \pmb n(t),\pmb d)\) is radiance field

    注：这里的 \(L(\pmb r(t), \pmb n(t),\pmb d)\) 相当于NeRF里的 \(c(\mathbf r(t),\pmb d)\)，只是增加了对边界法向量 \(\pmb n(t)\) 的依赖。

    "NeRF is heavily affected by the sampled point during intensity calculation."

    采样方法的问题：

    • NeRF的 "random uniform sample" 会造成 artifacts；
    • 若改成 "fixed uniform sample" 会造成另一种 artifacts；
